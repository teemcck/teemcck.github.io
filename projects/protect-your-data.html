Protect Your Data

A short-run topdown shooter about readable waves, quick retries, and boss checkpoints.

Itch GitHub

Overview

Protect Your Data is a topdown wave shooter built around short runs, clear enemy pressure, and three boss checkpoints that give the run a readable arc.

01 / Prototype

Movement and shooting

First goal: make the basic topdown loop feel clean enough to build on.

  • Player movement and aiming
  • Basic enemy pressure
  • Repeatable arena setup

02 / Combat Loop

Wave pacing

The run needed pressure without filling the screen with unreadable noise.

  • Enemy spawn timing
  • Recovery gaps between waves
  • Run structure for quick retries

03 / Boss Pass

Boss checkpoints

The bosses give the run a clearer shape than waves alone.

  • Three boss encounters
  • Separate pacing from normal waves
  • Future pass: upgrades and balance notes
